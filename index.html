Through General Assembly's Web Development Immersive and Data Science programs, I learned full stack Ruby on Rails development and data science (Natural Language Processing & Machine Learning) in Python using packages such as (PANDAS, NumPy, SciPy, NLTK, Scikit-Learn).

For two summers, I worked as a clinician at a intensive residential treatment facility for socially and emotionally disturbed children. I have over 1500 supervised clinical hours administering various therapeutic techniques including cognitive behavioral, positive behavior support, and crisis intervention.

During my second summer, I sought to optimize the token economy used to encourage pro-social behavior. I wanted to see if any particular positive behaviors were encourged more readily, if interpersonal relations between staff and patients effected reward frequency/amount and if there was any correlation between the thrice daily behavioral inventories scores and token acquisition.

As I was unable to control the total supply of tokens nor token distribution from staff outside of my clinical group, it became clear that I would have to control for inflation. I opted to use an auction to distribute goods starting with the least desirable goods and a closed bid auction for the most desirable goods. This way, patients with the fewest tokens were still able to get something and I was able to remove a large supply of tokens from the economy by encouraging maximum bids for highly desirable items.

While I was able to effectively manipulate the economy and keep inflation under control, I did not have enough time to take the data and come up with answers to my questions. I hope to one day be able to go back and finish this research
